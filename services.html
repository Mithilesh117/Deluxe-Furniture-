    What we offers

    Interior Design Consultation

    Deluxe Company provide interior design services where professional designers can assist customers in selecting furniture pieces, arranging layouts, and providing advice on color schemes, styles, and overall aesthetics.

    Space Planning

    We also offer space planning services to help customers optimize their living or office spaces. This involves analyzing the dimensions and layout of the area and suggesting furniture arrangements that maximize functionality and appeal.

    Delivery and Installation

    Deluxe company provide delivery services to transport the purchased furniture to the customer's location. Some companies may also offer installation services for items that require assembly, such as large furniture pieces or complex systems.

    Customization and Personalization

    We offer customization options to tailor furniture pieces to individual preferences. This can include choosing specific materials, finishes, colors, or even modifying dimensions to fit unique requirements.

    Financing and Payment Options

    To facilitate affordability, deluxe compnay furniture retailers provide financing options such as installment plans or leasing arrangements. This allows customers to spread the cost of their furniture purchases over time.

    Product Warranty and After-Sales Support

    Deluxe company often provide warranties on their products to guarantee quality and offer repair or replacement services for any defects or damages. They may also have customer service teams to assist with inquiries, troubleshooting, or addressing any issues that may arise after the purchase.

    Furniture Rental or Leasing

    Deluxe company offer rental or leasing services, particularly for temporary or short-term needs. This can be beneficial for individuals or businesses who require furniture for a specific period, such as staging a property for sale or setting up a temporary office space.

    Trade-In or Buy-Back Programs

    Deluxe Company have programs that allow customers to trade in their old furniture or sell it back to the company. This can provide convenience and potentially reduce the overall cost of new purchases.
